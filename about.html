About Us

Empowering Nepal's Future Through Technology

Robo Class Nepal is a group of qualified engineers and tech enthusiasts formed with the mindset and slogan "Let's Bring Technology Beyond The Text Books".

We primarily focus on various technical-learning aspects and skills development through practical approach rather than theoretical and bookish learning methods.

We strive to be committed to the community we are privileged to serve, with our dedicated team, talented faculty and staff. We take pride in having the most active and helpful group of partners, visionary parents, well-wishers, and friends.
